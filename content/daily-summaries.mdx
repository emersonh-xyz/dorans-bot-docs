# Daily Summaries
<br/>
<img src="images/misc/daily-summary.png" alt="Linking your account" width={400} height={400} />

Daily summaries are a way to keep track of your users Ranked Solo/Duo LP changes on a daily basis.

### Setting a channel for daily summaries

<Callout type='warning'>Daily summaries will deliver every night at 9:00pm EST. This is currently not configurable.</Callout>

To set a channel for daily summaries, type `/config daily-summaries [channel]`.

 Now play some games and the bot will track and send daily summaries in the channel you've selected!

### Viewing summary early

Alternatively, you can view the summary early by typing `/summary`
